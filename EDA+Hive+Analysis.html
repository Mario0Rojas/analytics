Create new columns:¶

timeOfDay

  • Morning: Time from midnight to midday(noon) 12:00 AM - 12:00 PM

  • Afternoon: Time from midday(noon) to evening 12:00 PM - 6:00 PM

  • Evening: Time from the end of the afternoon to midnight 6:00 PM - 12:00 AM

ampm

  • AM
  • PM
